### 🔷 Primitives

- ✅ Sphere
- ✅ Plane

### 🔷 Transformations

- ✅ Translation

### 🔷 Lighting

- ✅ Directional light
- ✅ Ambient light

- ✅ Load from external file
- ✅ Camera positioning

- ✅ No graphical display required
